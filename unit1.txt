Unit 1: Data Protection
🔹 Definition
Cybersecurity is the practice of safeguarding digital assets from threats.

🔹 Why It Matters
Global cybercrime cost:
$6 trillion (2021)
$10 trillion (projected)
Targets:
Individuals
Small businesses
Enterprises
Governments

Key Risks
    IoT vulnerabilities
    Increasing attack surface
    Undetected breaches

Real-World Examples
    National infrastructure attacks
    Corporate data leaks
    Supply chain compromises
    IoT-based intrusions

Prevention + Awareness = First line of defense
